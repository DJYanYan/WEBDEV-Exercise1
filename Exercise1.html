We went to a museum resort name The Balanghai Hotel and they had a sign saying:

February 11, 2022

This is our friend's Abaca Farm located near Bukidnon. We made sure to pack all the equipment necessary for a hike;

  • Water
  • Hats and Caps
  • Trekking Pole
  • Food

Just the essentials. It didn't take too long to tour the entire farm.
